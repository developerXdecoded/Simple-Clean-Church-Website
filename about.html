APOSTLE

Benny L.

DOZIER

Senior Pastor

Apostle Dozier is the Senior Pastor of Power & Light Evangelistic Church. He has a strong gift of leadership upon his life and a passion for teaching leaders, pastors and other apostles. He also flows heavily in the gift of prophecy and is a marketplace and pulpit preacher. Under the direction of the Holy Spirit Apostle Dozier established the Apostolic Power International Network. This Network has been commissioned to restore the office of the apostle and the apostolic church, to help leaders and their ministries build the kingdom of God within the Nuclear Church and in the marketplace.


This apostolic trailblazer has established a school and is a successful businessman. He oversees the businesses that are a part of the A.P.I.N. locations throughout the Midwest region of the United States. Apostle Dozier received his Bachelor of Ministry degree from Wagner Leadership Institute and is currently pursuing his Masters degree. He has been married to his wife Prophetess Gerry Dozier for over thirty five years. They reside in the Chicagoland area and have two sons, one daughter-in-law, and three grandchildren.

About Our

Prophetess.

Prophetess Gerry Dozier is the Pastor of Power & Light Church of Gary, in Gary, Indiana. She has a great calling and passion to minister, to motivate and to help women. Prophetess Gerry oversees an outreach ministry entitled, Women of Power Purpose and Destiny that specializes in preparing women for their work in ministry and the edification of the body of Christ. She is also called to minister to pastors’ wives. Prophetess Gerry operates in the gifts of discerning of spirits, the word of wisdom, and the gift of exhortation. She has the anointing to minister, inspire, and encourage individuals at no matter what level they are on or their walk of life. This modern-day Deborah has established a school and is also a successful businesswoman and active community leader.


In addition, Prophetess Gerry oversees an outreach ministry entitled, ‘Women in Ministry’ that specializes in preparing women for their work in ministry and the edification of the body of Christ. Prophetess Gerry received her Bachelor of Ministry degree from Vision International University. She has been married, for over thirty-five years, to Apostle Benny L. Dozier Sr., the Senior Pastor of Power & Light Evangelistic Church, in South Holland, Illinois. They reside in the Chicagoland area and have two sons, one daughter-in-law, and three grandchildren.
